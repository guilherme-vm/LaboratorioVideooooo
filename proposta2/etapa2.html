"And what then is the written word?"

Keywords: Media, Fake News, Notion of time.

With the help of the audio build in ETAPA 1 this video was assembled to represent a linear desconstrunction.
"The beginning of it all". Where does it start? What came first? The egg, or the chicken? What is this beginning? One only possesses the notion of time passing when looking back, it could be yesterday, it could be a few months ago, it could be the building of ancient civilizations.
Time and life go on, following one path in multiple directions, straight forward directions and tree-branch directions, using the empty space to "help" absorb the content seen. I decided to include clips such as atomic explosions, the pyramids, cell splitting and the news, to emphasize the time passing, the destruction and yet, construction and multiplication leading forwards, learning from the past, creating a visual chaos spread along a timeline, more and more eye consuming.
